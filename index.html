Hi! I am Kwok Lung Fan(Fan is my surname, you can just call me Jason). I am a year three undergraduate student at The University of Hong Kong studying physics and decision analytics. My research interests are gamma-ray astronomy, astroparticle, data mining and space science. I am also interested in science outreach. I am currently working with Dr. Philips Yu and Dr. Parkinson on background rejection of High-Altitude Water Cherenkov Observatory(HAWC) with machine learning. I spent two months at the University of Maryland, College Park working on the background rejection project and I was supervised by Prof. Jordan Goodman. I worked with Dr. Pablo Parkinson on Fermi LAT unassociated gamma-ray sources classification and pulsar search before the HAWC project(Help FAST team found their first MSP PSR J0318+0253!). I was a summer research fellow at HKU supervised by Dr. Meng Su and continue working with Dr. Su on gamma-ray astronomy. I also worked with Dr. CK Chui from the Department of Computer Science on programming teaching and Dr. Z. Qu from the Department of Mathematics on numerical simulation of convex optimization.
